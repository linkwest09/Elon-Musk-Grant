Contact & Support FAQs

❓ Common Questions
💡 Expert Answers
Image Placeholder

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?
Email inquiries receive responses within 24 hours during business days. WhatsApp and Telegram messages are typically answered within 2-5 minutes during business hours. Phone support provides immediate assistance Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM PST.
Can I schedule a consultation call before applying?
Yes! We offer free 30-minute consultation calls to discuss your project ideas and determine the best approach for your application. Contact us via email or phone to schedule your session.
Do you provide application assistance in languages other than English?
Our support team can assist in English, French, and Spanish. For other languages, we recommend using our email support where we can utilize translation services to ensure clear communication.
What information should I prepare before contacting support?
Have your project concept, estimated budget, timeline, and any specific questions ready. If you're asking about an existing application, please include your reference number or contact details used in the application.
Can I visit your offices in person?
Yes, office visits are welcome by appointment only. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule a meeting with our local grant coordinators.
Is there a cost for consultation or application assistance?
All consultation services, application assistance, and support are completely free. We believe in removing barriers to innovation and provide comprehensive support at no charge.
